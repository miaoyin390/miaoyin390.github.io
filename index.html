Welcome to Noodle Lab

We are a dynamic research group in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at UT Arlington (UTA), located at Dallas-Fort Worth Metroplex. Our aim is to build the next-generation efficient computing engine for democratizing artificial intelligence to real-world on-device applications. To reach this goal, we develop the software/hardware co-optimized AI systems based on higher-order tensor decomposition and optimization algorithms.

Previous Next

Specifically, we leverage mathematical tools to fundamentally solve the efficiency problems and improve the task accuracy. We also develop the corresponding hardware/GPU accelerators for our specific software designs.

Our lab is located in the Engineering Research Building at UTA main campus. We are equipped with a high-performance computing server with 8 A100 cards. We are open to collaborate and exchange ideas with external researchers.

We are looking for passionate and self-motivated PhD students to join our lab! See openings.
