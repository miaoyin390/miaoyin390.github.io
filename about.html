Miao Yin

Biography

Dr. Miao Yin is a Tenure-Track Assistant Professor of Computer Science at the University of Texas at Arlington (UTA). He received his Ph.D. at Rutgers University, during which he conducted research at Google with Dr. Boqing Gong, building the efficient YouTube video quality assessment framework. Previously, he obtained his M.E. and B.E. in EE from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China and Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications, respectively.
